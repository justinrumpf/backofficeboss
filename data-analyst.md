# Data Analyst

**Ask a question, get an answer — grounded in your real numbers.**

> _Replaces / augments: Embedded data analyst_

Most teams either wait days for a report or never get the answer at all. The Data Analyst keeps your numbers current every night, watches them for things no one put on a dashboard yet, answers questions in plain English, and writes a one-page brief on the whole business every morning.

---

## Everything it does

### Keeps every number fresh
- Rebuilds your performance views **every night**, across stores, brands, employees, helpdesk, and hiring.
- Every comparison to last year is **fiscal-aware** — it lines up the right weeks, not just "this date a year ago."
- The result: your dashboards open **instantly** the next morning, already current.

### Watches for what isn't on anyone's dashboard
Five kinds of detectors run against the fresh numbers every night:
- **Outliers** — a value that's way off this store's normal range.
- **Trends** — something steadily climbing or falling over the past two weeks.
- **Thresholds** — a number crossing a line you care about (e.g. discounts over a set %).
- **Cross-team signals** — patterns that only show up when you join data across teams (like a store that's busy but understaffed).
- **Drop-offs** — a funnel leaking, like resumes that stop converting or tickets that stall.

Each finding comes with a **headline, a recommendation, a severity, and a confidence** — and respects your dismissals, so something you've said "that's expected" about won't keep nagging you.

### Answers questions in plain English
- A **question box** where anyone can ask: *"What was sales by brand last week?"* or *"Which store had the most flagged calls?"*
- Answers are drawn **only from your real data** — it shows you the underlying table alongside the plain-English answer.
- Built on a **curated set of trusted questions**, so answers are reliable rather than made up.
- Keeps a **history** of past questions and answers.

### Writes a daily executive brief
- Every morning at 10 AM it produces a **one-page "state of the business"** — per-team strengths, watch lists, suggested goals, and emerging cross-team trends.
- An **insight ticker** cycles the day's top findings on the home page, each one clickable.
- You can **pin** the charts and numbers you care about to the top.

### Acts as the shared backbone
- The Data Analyst's nightly numbers are what **every other Boss relies on** — so operations goals, strategy scores, and labor analysis all read from one consistent source of truth.
