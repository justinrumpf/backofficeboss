# Data Analyst

**Ask a question, get an answer — grounded in your real numbers.**

> _Replaces / augments: Embedded data analyst_

Most teams either wait days for a report or never get the answer at all. The Data Analyst keeps your numbers current every night and lets anyone ask a question in plain English — with answers drawn straight from your own data, not guesswork.

## What it does for you

- **Keeps the numbers fresh.** Every night it rebuilds your store, brand, and employee performance views, complete with same-period-last-year comparisons.
- **Answers plain-English questions.** "How did Store 12 do last week versus last year?" — asked in normal language, answered from your real data.
- **Spots the story in the numbers**, automatically flagging:
  - Outliers that don't fit the pattern
  - Trends building over time
  - Thresholds being crossed
  - Conversion drop-offs
  - Cross-team signals (like a store that's busy but understaffed)
- **Delivers a daily executive brief.** Each morning, a short written summary of how the business is doing — strengths, watch-list, and where to focus.
