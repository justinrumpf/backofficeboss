# Loss Prevention Boss

**A tireless investigator watching every transaction.**

> _Replaces / augments: Loss-prevention investigator_

Shrink and internal theft hide in the noise of thousands of routine transactions. Loss Prevention Boss reviews them all, scores each salesperson's risk against their peers, and runs live checks for the specific patterns that signal a problem — so you investigate the right people, with evidence in hand.

## What it does for you

- **Scores risk fairly, against peers.** Every salesperson is rated relative to their cohort, so seasonal swings and busy stores don't create false alarms.
- **Watches the signals that matter:** discount rates, returns, voids, no-sales, large unverified cash buys, and accounting variances.
- **Runs live fraud detectors**, including:
  - **Buys with no items sold** — a classic red flag
  - **Large cash buys without ID verification**
  - **Cross-checks against the day's accounting**
- **Schedules fair, unpredictable audits.** It picks who to spot-check in a way that can't be gamed — while guaranteeing everyone is reviewed on a regular cycle.
- **Builds the case for you.** When someone is flagged, you get the supporting transactions, not just a name.
