# Helpdesk Boss

**Your helpdesk manager and agent coach — on duty 24/7.**

> _Replaces / augments: Helpdesk manager + agent coach_

Every support ticket and phone call your team handles carries signal: how well the issue was solved, how the customer felt, and whether it's the same problem cropping up again and again. Reviewing all of it by hand is impossible. Helpdesk Boss reads **every** ticket and call, and tells you what's worth your attention.

## What it does for you

- **Reads and scores every interaction.** Each ticket and call is rated on how knowledgeable the response was, whether it actually resolved the issue, and the customer's sentiment.
- **Backs up every score with evidence.** When something scores low, you see the exact moment from the conversation that explains why — no guessing.
- **Catches recurring problems early.** When the same issue hits the same location more than once in a day, Helpdesk Boss flags it as a pattern and drafts the outreach to get ahead of it.
- **Grows your knowledge base automatically.** Solved problems become reusable, verified answers, so the next person doesn't have to reinvent the fix.
- **Coaches your team.** Spot which agents are excelling and which need support, with specific examples instead of vague feedback.
