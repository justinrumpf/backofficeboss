# Frequently asked questions

### Is this off-the-shelf software I can download?
No. BackOfficeBoss is **custom-built for each business.** The Bosses you see in this documentation were engineered for a multi-store resale operation. Yours are built around your roles, your reports, and your rules. We can adapt these as a starting point or design something entirely new.

### Will I have to change how my business works to use it?
No — that's the whole point. Most software forces you to bend your processes to fit it. BackOfficeBoss is built the other way around: it's shaped to how your team already works, including the rules and edge cases that only live in someone's head today.

### Where does my data live? Is it safe?
Your data stays **in your building, on your own hardware** — a quiet, desk-sized computer in your office. The AI that reasons over your data runs locally too. Nothing is sent to a third-party cloud. No outside service reads your customers, finances, or staff information.

### Does the AI act on its own?
Only when you let it. Every action that reaches the outside world — an email, a filing, an update — starts as a **draft you approve.** Nothing happens without your say-so. As you confirm the system is getting it right, you can choose to let it handle more on its own.

### What does it cost?
Because every solution is custom, pricing depends on what you need automated and the value it creates. The honest way to think about it: a tailored, in-house system is a **serious, five-figure investment that you own outright** — and it's measured against the labor hours it gives back to you, year after year. Most clients find the math makes itself. Try the [savings estimator on our website](https://backofficeboss.ai/#savings) to see what your back office is costing today.

### What kinds of businesses is it for?
It was built and proven in multi-store retail and resale, but the approach fits any business that runs on repeatable back-office work — real estate and insurance brokerages, law firms, medical and dental offices, retail owners, and small businesses generally. If your team does it every day, it's likely automatable.

### How long does it take to build?
That depends on scope. We start with a short discovery conversation to understand your back office, then propose a tailored plan. Solutions are built and rolled out in stages, so you start seeing value early.

### Do I need a technical team to run it?
No. It's designed to be operated by the people who already run your back office. You work in one simple interface; the complexity stays under the hood.

### How do I get started?
Start with a no-pressure conversation about your back office.
