# Strategy Boss

**The view from 30,000 feet — refreshed every week.**

> _Replaces / augments: Chief strategist + CEO scoreboard_

The hardest part of running a multi-location business is seeing the whole board at once: which risks are building, which brands are pulling ahead, and the problems you don't even know to look for. Strategy Boss watches everything the other Bosses see and distills it into a clear, ranked picture for leadership.

## What it does for you

- **Connects the dots across the business.** It reviews signals from every other Boss — accounting, helpdesk, loss prevention, operations, labor — and pulls them into one risk picture.
- **Ranks what matters most.** Aging financial issues, recurring support problems, high-severity loss-prevention cases, stalled goals, and more — ordered by how much they should worry you.
- **Surfaces blind spots.** A regular "what are we missing?" sweep flags issues you didn't think to ask about — like quietly eroding pricing or aging inventory.
- **Scores and ranks your brands.** A composite scoreboard shows which parts of the business are winning and which need attention.
- **Proposes the next big goals.** Each quarter it suggests strategic priorities, grounded in what the data is actually telling you.
